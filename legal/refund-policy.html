Refund and Return Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2023

1. Introduction

At SkyBetPro, we value your satisfaction with our app development services. This Refund and Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding refunds and the remediation process for our services.

2. Services Rendered

SkyBetPro provides custom app development services. Our work is considered a service rather than a physical product. As such, traditional "returns" do not apply. However, we are committed to client satisfaction and have established the following refund policy.

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Initial Consultation and Deposit

If you decide not to proceed with our services after the initial consultation but before any development work has begun, we will refund 75% of your initial deposit. The remaining 25% covers our time for consultation, requirement analysis, and preliminary planning.

3.2 Development in Progress

Once development work has begun:

  • If you cancel the project within the first week of development, a 50% refund of your initial payment may be issued at our discretion, based on the amount of work already completed.
  • For projects canceled after the first week of development, refunds will be prorated based on the percentage of work completed and expenses incurred.
  • No refunds will be issued for completed project milestones that have been approved by the client.

3.3 Completed Projects

For completed projects:

  • SkyBetPro offers a 30-day warranty period after project completion to fix bugs and issues related to the original project scope at no additional cost.
  • Once a project is completed and delivered, no refunds will be issued unless the delivered application fails to meet the specifications agreed upon in the contract.
  • If the application does not meet agreed-upon specifications, we will first attempt to remedy the issues. Refunds will only be considered if we are unable to fulfill the contractual obligations after reasonable attempts to correct the issues.

4. Exclusions

The following situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • Changes in project requirements after development has begun
  • Requests for features or functionality not specified in the original project scope
  • Delays caused by the client's failure to provide necessary information, feedback, or approvals
  • Issues arising from third-party services or APIs that are beyond our control
  • Changes in business strategy or market conditions affecting the client's need for the application

5. Remedy Process

If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our services:

  • Contact us within 30 days of project completion.
  • Clearly state the issues and how they deviate from the agreed-upon specifications.
  • Allow us a reasonable opportunity to address and resolve the issues.
  • If resolution cannot be achieved, a partial refund may be considered based on the nature and extent of the unresolved issues.

6. Refund Processing

All approved refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days and will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction, unless otherwise agreed upon.

7. Force Majeure

SkyBetPro is not liable for any failure or delay in performance due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, natural disasters, pandemic, terrorism, riot, or governmental actions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund and Return Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund and Return Policy, please contact us at billing@skybetpro.com.
